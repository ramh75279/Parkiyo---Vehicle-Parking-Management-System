Registration

Visit the Register page and fill in your name, email, phone number, password, and gender. Self-registration creates a standard user account. Admin accounts can only be created by an existing administrator through the user management panel.

No. Each email address must be unique in the system. If you try to register with an existing email, you'll receive a duplicate email error. Use the Forgot Password page to recover access to your existing account.

Admin accounts must be created by an existing administrator. They cannot be self-registered. Contact your facility's system administrator to have an admin role assigned or a new admin account created on your behalf.

lock_open

Login & Access

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and Parkiyo will send password reset instructions. If the email exists in the system, you'll receive a secure reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.

Parkiyo supports QR code-based entry on the login screen. A unique QR code is generated that you can scan from a connected device to authenticate. This is ideal for fast operator logins at shared terminals where typing credentials is inconvenient.

Login redirects are role-based. Administrators are taken to the Admin Dashboard with full system controls. Standard users are taken to the User Dashboard with operational tools for vehicle entry, exit, payments, and receipts.

Payments & Wallet

Fees are calculated automatically based on the total parking duration when a vehicle exits. The system uses the configured hourly or flat rate for each slot category. The amount due is displayed before payment is confirmed.

Yes. After payment is confirmed, a digital receipt is generated automatically. It includes your ticket number, plate, slot, entry/exit times, duration, and total amount paid. Receipts are accessible from your receipt history and can be printed or downloaded.

calendar_month

Reservations

Advance reservations can be made for any future date and time where slots are available. You select your preferred date, time, and slot type. The system checks real-time availability before confirming. Reservations are visible to the admin and attached to your profile.

Reservation management policies are configured by your facility administrator. Depending on settings, no-show reservations may be automatically released after a grace period, making the slot available for other drivers. Contact your facility admin for specific cancellation and no-show policies.

directions_car

Entry & Exit

An operator enters the vehicle's plate number, selects or assigns a slot, and confirms entry. The system records the entry time automatically and generates a parking ticket. The vehicle session becomes active and appears in live operations on the admin dashboard.

Search for the vehicle by plate number or ticket ID. The system shows the session duration and calculates the fee. After confirming payment, the slot is freed and a receipt is generated. The vehicle session moves from active to exited in the operations log.
