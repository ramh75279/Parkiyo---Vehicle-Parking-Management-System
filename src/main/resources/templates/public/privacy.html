Overview

Parkiyo ("we", "our", "the platform") is a premium parking management system. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using Parkiyo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to data minimisation — we collect only what is necessary to operate the platform effectively and securely.

database

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering and using the system, as well as data generated automatically during platform use.

Account Information

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Gender (for profile records)
  • Encrypted password (never stored in plain text)
  • Account role (admin or user)

Operational Data

  • Vehicle plate numbers entered into the system
  • Slot assignments and parking session records
  • Entry and exit timestamps
  • Reservation records

Payment Data

  • Payment amounts and transaction records
  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Receipt identifiers and payment status
analytics

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to operate, maintain, and improve the Parkiyo platform. Specific uses include:

  • Authenticating users and managing access control
  • Processing vehicle entry, exit, and parking sessions
  • Calculating fees and processing payments
  • Generating parking receipts and records
  • Providing administrators with operational reports and analytics
  • Maintaining audit logs for security and compliance
  • Sending system notifications relevant to your account

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

shield

Security

Parkiyo implements industry-standard security measures to protect all user and operational data. These include:

  • Encrypted password storage using secure hashing
  • Role-based access control preventing unauthorised data access
  • Audit logging of all sensitive system actions
  • System health monitoring with real-time alerts

Despite these measures, no digital system is completely invulnerable. We encourage users to use strong passwords and log out from shared terminals after use.

schedule

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active and as long as required for operational and compliance purposes. Payment and parking records are retained for a minimum period to support auditing and dispute resolution.

Account data may be deleted upon request. Contact the administrator of your facility or Parkiyo support to initiate an account deletion request.

For privacy-related queries, data requests, or concerns about how your information is handled, reach out to our team.
